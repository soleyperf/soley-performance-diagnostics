Pensacola Mobile Motorcycle Diagnostics Pricing
Updated: April 2026

Positioning
Mobile diagnostics should save the customer a tow, reduce shop wait time, and give a clear repair path. These prices are built to stay competitive with Pensacola mobile diagnostic calls while still charging properly for skilled motorcycle electrical, fuel, and no-start troubleshooting.

Core Mobile Diagnostic Visit - $95
Best for: check-engine/FI light, rough idle, poor running, basic no-start triage, battery/charging concerns, post-storage issues, and "I need to know what is wrong."
Includes:
- Travel within the core Pensacola service area
- Symptom verification
- Code scan when the bike supports it
- Battery and charging quick check when relevant
- Visual inspection of obvious failure points
- Clear findings and next-step repair estimate
Time included: up to 45 minutes on site.

Advanced Diagnostic / No-Start / Electrical - $125 first hour
Best for: intermittent electrical faults, crank/no-start, fuel/ignition problems, sensor faults, charging failures, wiring issues, parasitic draw, and bikes that need deeper testing.
Includes: the first hour of testing.
Additional time: $90/hour, billed in 30-minute increments only with customer approval.

Battery, Starter, and Charging Check - $65
30-minute focused check for weak battery, starter/solenoid symptoms, charging output, loose connections, and basic cable condition.
If the problem requires deeper tracing, apply the $65 toward a Core or Advanced Diagnostic the same visit.

Pre-Purchase Motorcycle Inspection - $125
On-site buyer inspection with start/run check, charging check, leak/fluids visual, brakes/tires/chain/sprockets, controls/lights, obvious crash or neglect signs, and a plain-language buy/no-buy risk summary.
Add compression test when accessible: +$50.

Post-Storage Start-Up / Quick Tune Assessment - $85
For bikes that have been sitting. Includes fuel condition check, battery/charging quick check, basic air/fuel/spark direction, and a restart plan.
Repairs, carb cleaning, parts, fluids, and teardown are quoted separately.

Tune-Up / Maintenance Labor - starting at $140
Common work: plugs, air filter, oil/filter, chain adjustment, battery install, and basic inspection.
Final price depends on bike access, parts, fluids, and whether fairings/tank removal are needed.

Repair Labor After Diagnosis - $90/hour
Quoted before work begins. Larger jobs can be flat-rated when the scope is clear.

Travel
- Included: Pensacola core, West Pensacola, Ferry Pass, Warrington, Myrtle Grove, Bellview, Ensley.
- Nearby add-on: Pace, Milton, Gulf Breeze, Cantonment, Perdido, Beulah: typically +$25 to $45 depending on distance and schedule.
- Longer trips: quoted before booking.

Conversion Offer
If the customer approves a repair over $250 within 14 days, credit $50 of the diagnostic visit toward the repair labor.

Scheduling / Policy
- No work starts without customer approval.
- Parts, fluids, shop supplies, and specialty items are extra unless stated.
- Customer must provide a safe, legal work area with room around the motorcycle.
- Same-day or after-hours appointments may include a $35 convenience fee.
- If the bike is unsafe to run or needs shop-only equipment, the diagnostic still provides findings and recommended next steps.
